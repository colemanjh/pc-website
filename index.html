JC's Standards

Three Pillars of Every Build

01

Silence by Design

Acoustic engineering is not an afterthought — it is the foundation of every fan curve, cooler selection, and case choice to optimize and deliver a near perfectly crafted build.

02

Long-Term Stability

Each build undergoes a full day of stress testing. Components are matched for thermal compatibility, power delivery headroom, and signal integrity. Nothing ships until perfection is confirmed.

03

Enduring Excellence

By selecting platforms with confirmed upgrade paths — like AMD AM5 — and premium-grade components, every JC build is engineered to remain relevant and upgradeable for years beyond delivery.

How It Works

The Commission Process

01

Consultation & Specification

We will meet with you to understand your workflow, environment, and aspirations, using tailored crafted specification for only your component specific needs.

02

Component Sourcing & Vetting

Every part is sourced directly from authorized distributors. Batch numbers are verified and components are inspected before the assembly begins. No refurbished. No grey market. No exceptions.

03

Hand Assembly & Cable Artistry

JC assembles every machine part personally — routing cables with precision, optimizing airflow channels, and applying premium thermal compounds. The interior is finished to gallery standard.

04

72-Hour Stress Testing

Before packaging, every build is run a full hour per component for thermal and stability testing. The CPU, GPU, RAM, and storage are all verified under maximum load. Temperatures, frequencies, and errors are logged and reviewed.

05

Delivery & Lifetime Support

Your machine is delivered with full documentation, build photos, and test logs. JC provides lifetime support for every JC Puzzling Crafted build, because a relationship with your computor should last.
